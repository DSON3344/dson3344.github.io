About Me

Hi there! I’m DSON, a multimedia design student with a deep passion for crafting engaging and meaningful digital experiences. My expertise lies in video editing, UI/UX design, and I also enjoy diving into the worlds of animation and game design.

I actively share my creative journey across multiple platforms, including YouTube, Bilibili, Instagram, TikTok, Xiaohongshu (Rednote), and X (Twitter). Among them, YouTube and Bilibili are my primary spaces for content creation. If you're a fellow fan of Call of Duty Mobile, let’s connect and explore its exciting universe together!

My mission is to transform creativity into work that resonates, inspires, and connects. Feel free to browse my portfolio or reach out through social media—I’d love to share my passion with you!
