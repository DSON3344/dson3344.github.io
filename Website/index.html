About Me

Hi! I’m Chang Dick Shen, but you can call me DSON. I'm a multimedia design student with a deep passion for crafting engaging and meaningful digital experiences. My expertise includes video editing, UI/UX design, and I also enjoy exploring the creative worlds of animation and game design.

I actively share my creative content across multiple platforms, including YouTube, Bilibili, Instagram, TikTok, Xiaohongshu (Rednote), and X (Twitter). Among these platforms, YouTube and Bilibili are my primary spaces for sharing my creative work.

My mission is to transform creativity into work that resonates, inspires, and connects. Feel free to browse my portfolio or reach out through social media. I’d love to share my passion with you!
