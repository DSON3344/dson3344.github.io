Ocean Guardian

Ocean Guardian is a mobile app that raises awareness about marine pollution and encourages users to take action. It provides ecosystem notifications, promotes volunteer activities, and lets users share achievements. The app aims to build a community that helps restore and protect our oceans, making them cleaner and more vibrant.

MnOwner

MnOwner helps solve Malaysia’s double parking problem by reminding drivers to park responsibly. It reduces conflicts, saves time, and creates a more considerate parking culture, while supporting smoother traffic and community convenience.
